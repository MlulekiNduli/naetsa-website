National Executive Committee

Chairperson:
Thulebona Cele

Brief History: Thulebona Cele has a long history of leadership and service within NaTeSA. He began his journey in 2017 as the Deputy Secretary of the UKZN Westville branch. In 2022, he was co-opted as a member of the National Executive Committee (NEC), serving from May to October of that year. Thulebona was later elected as the NEC Secretary General, a role he held from October 2022 to August 2024. His leadership and dedication to NaTeSA were further recognized when he was elected as the National Chairperson in August 2024, a position he currently holds.

Qualifications: Bachelor's Degree in Optometry (UKZN)

Personal Achievements:

  • Opened Amaqaphelo Eye Care practice in Mnambithi.
  • Opened Amaqaphelo Eye Care practice in Durban.
  • Created 5 job opportunities through these practices.

Add Member:
Khanyisani Makhoba

Brief History:Khanyisani was born in Mandeni Sundumbili and grew up in Richards Bay, KwaZulu-Natal. In 2012, he began studying for a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering at the University of KwaZulu-Natal (UKZN). From 2015 to 2016, he served as the branch chairperson for NaTeSA at UKZN Howard College Campus. After completing his studies, Khanyisani gained valuable experience working for various companies, including Richards Bay Minerals, starting in 2017. He is currently working towards obtaining professional registration with the Engineering Council of South Africa (ECSA) as an Electrical Engineer.

Qualifications: PhD Electrical Engineering

Personal Achievements:

  • Awarded as one of the Top 5 Matric Achievers by uThungulu District Municipality (2011).

  • Recognized by The Golden Key International Society as one of the top 15% performing postgraduate students at the University of KwaZulu-Natal (2020).
